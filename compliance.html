The Framework

How We Handle Legality

Product legality in the United States is complicated. The same item can be fully legal in one state, restricted in another, and outright banned in a third — sometimes with no federal guidance at all. Mainstream platforms respond to this complexity by banning everything that might cause a problem. We respond differently.

We built a compliance framework that filters products by buyer location at checkout. When you provide a shipping address, we verify that the products in your cart are legal for delivery to that state. If a product isn't legal where you're shipping, it won't process.

01
Seller Tags Products

When a seller lists a product, they tag it with the states where it is legal to ship. They are contractually responsible for the accuracy of these tags under our Seller Agreement.

02
Buyer Provides Address

At checkout, the buyer provides a shipping address. We use the state from that address to filter which products can be purchased in that transaction.

03
Compliance Verified

If a product is not tagged as legal in the buyer's state, the transaction will not process. Buyers are also responsible for knowing their local laws.

Responsibilities

Who Is Responsible for What

Compliance is a shared responsibility between Silque Road, our sellers, and our buyers. Here is exactly how we divide it.

Silque Road
  • Vetting sellers before approval
  • Maintaining the state compliance filter at checkout
  • Reviewing product listings for obvious compliance issues
  • Removing products flagged as non-compliant
  • Updating compliance tags as laws change
  • Providing this compliance documentation
Sellers
  • Accurately tagging which states they can ship to
  • Staying current on product legality in their shipping states
  • Labeling products correctly (research use, veterinary, etc.)
  • Complying with all applicable shipping laws
  • Updating their state tags when laws change
  • Accuracy of all product descriptions and claims
Buyer Responsibility

Buyers are responsible for knowing and complying with the laws of their jurisdiction. Silque Road's compliance filter is a tool to assist — it is not a guarantee of legality in every local jurisdiction. Some municipalities have laws that differ from state law. Buyers should verify local ordinances before purchasing. Silque Road assumes no liability for purchases made in violation of local law.

Reporting

Flagging Compliance Issues

If you believe a product is listed in violation of your state's laws, or if a seller is shipping to states where their product is prohibited, we want to know. Contact us at info@silqueroad.com with the product name and the specific concern. We investigate all compliance reports within 48 hours.

Sellers found to be deliberately misrepresenting state legality will be removed from the platform immediately and permanently.
