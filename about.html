The Problem

The Shelf Keeps Moving

Something changed in American commerce. Legal products — products sold openly, taxed like any other good, protected by state law — started disappearing from mainstream platforms. Not because they were illegal. Because they were inconvenient.

Kratom. Peptides. THCA. Ivermectin. Methylene blue. Flavored nicotine. Delta-8. Fenbendazole. Raw dairy. Each one legal in most of the country. Each one systematically de-platformed, payment-processed out of existence, or buried so deep in search results it may as well not exist.

The sellers didn't break the law. The buyers didn't break the law. But the platforms made a different calculation — that the reputational risk of carrying these products outweighed the revenue. And so they moved the shelf.

"Legal doesn't mean available. We built Silque Road to change that."

The Solution

The Open Market, Finally

Silque Road is a marketplace built specifically for legal products that mainstream platforms won't carry. Every product listed here is legal under applicable federal and state law. Every seller is vetted before they can list. Every buyer can check out anonymously if they choose.

We built the payment infrastructure that mainstream processors refuse to touch. We built the compliance framework that makes state-by-state legality clear. We built the seller tools that let independent operators run a real business without fear of waking up to a suspended account.

We are not a gray market. We are not a loophole. We are what commerce was always supposed to be — open, legal, and free from the politics of platform gatekeepers.

Our Principles

What We Stand For

01
Legal Means Legal

If a product is legal in your state, you should be able to buy it. We don't add extra gates beyond what the law requires.

02
Anonymous by Default

Buyers should not have to justify their legal purchases to anyone. Anonymous checkout is a feature, not a workaround.

03
No Deplatforming

We do not suspend sellers for selling legal products. If you're operating legally, your store stays open. Period.

04
Seller First

Sellers keep 90% of every sale. We take 10% to run the platform. Payouts go out weekly in crypto to whatever wallet you choose.

05
Crypto Native

Crypto payments aren't an afterthought — they're the foundation. 8 cryptocurrencies accepted. Full anonymity for buyers who want it.

06
Compliance Built In

State compliance is our responsibility, not yours. Products are filtered by buyer location so you never accidentally sell across a legal line.

The Name

Why Silque Road

The Silk Road was history's greatest open marketplace — a 4,000-mile network of trade routes connecting East to West, where goods, ideas, and commerce flowed freely across borders that governments tried to control. It operated for over a thousand years because the demand for open trade is more durable than any attempt to restrict it.

Silque Road is named for that tradition — the belief that markets want to be open, that buyers and sellers have a right to transact freely, and that the platforms trying to control what you can buy and sell are on the wrong side of history.

We spelled it differently on purpose. This is something new.
