Shipping Policy

How orders move from vetted sellers to your door — discreetly, trackably, and on time.

Last Updated: April 15, 2026

Section 1

How Orders Ship

Silque Road is a marketplace. Each product is shipped directly by the vetted independent seller who listed it — not by Silque Road, LLC. When you place an order, the platform notifies the seller, collects payment, and the seller is responsible for fulfillment, packaging, carrier selection, and delivery.

This means shipping times, carriers, and packaging may vary by seller. Each seller is required to meet the platform's minimum standards (listed below) and to ship every paid order within the stated processing window.

Section 2

Processing & Delivery Times

The following are platform-wide standards. Individual sellers may ship faster.

Stage Timeframe
Order confirmation Immediate (after crypto or card payment confirms)
Seller processing 1–3 business days
Domestic shipping (US) 3–7 business days after dispatch
Tracking provided By email within 24 hours of dispatch

Crypto payments require on-chain confirmation before the order is released to the seller. This typically takes 10–60 minutes depending on the network.

Section 3

Discreet Packaging

All sellers on Silque Road are required to ship in plain, unbranded packaging. The outer packaging will not display product names, category indicators, or any markings that suggest the contents.

Return addresses on parcels will reflect the seller's fulfillment location, not Silque Road, LLC. Many sellers use generic LLC names or fulfillment-service return addresses for additional privacy.

Why discreet packaging matters: The products on this platform are legal — but discretion is part of the experience. We require it from every seller.

Section 4

Tracking Your Order

If you provided an email at checkout, you'll receive tracking information by email as soon as the seller dispatches your order. You can also check order status anytime at silqueroad.com/order-status using your Order ID.

If you checked out fully anonymously and provided no email, you'll need your Order ID (shown on the confirmation screen at checkout) to look up status. We strongly recommend saving your Order ID — without an email on file, it is the only way to track or follow up on your order.

Section 5

Shipping Restrictions

Some products listed on Silque Road are subject to state-level shipping restrictions. Sellers are responsible for refusing to ship to states where their products are not legal. Examples include, but are not limited to:

  • Raw dairy: May not be shipped to states that prohibit interstate sale of raw milk products.
  • Kratom: Will not be shipped to states or municipalities where kratom is restricted or banned (currently includes parts of AL, AR, IN, RI, VT, WI, and certain cities).
  • Tobacco & nicotine: Subject to PACT Act compliance and state excise tax requirements; sellers ship only where legally permitted.
  • Hemp-derived cannabinoid products: Shipped only to states where the specific product remains legal.
  • Weapons-adjacent items: Shipping restrictions vary by item and destination state.

If a seller cannot lawfully ship to your address, your order will be refunded in the cryptocurrency or card you paid with, less any non-recoverable network fees.

Section 6

Lost, Stolen & Damaged Packages

Once a package is marked delivered by the carrier, responsibility transfers to the buyer. We recommend shipping to an address where someone is present to receive the package, or to a P.O. Box or parcel locker.

If your package is lost in transit (carrier shows movement, then nothing for 10+ business days), contact us with your Order ID. We will work directly with the seller to resolve the issue, which may include a replacement shipment or a full refund.

If your package arrives damaged, photograph the outer packaging and contents within 48 hours of delivery and email info@silqueroad.com with your Order ID and photos. Damage claims must be opened within 7 days of delivery.

Section 7

International Shipping

At this time, Silque Road sellers ship within the United States only. We do not currently support international addresses, APO/FPO addresses, or shipping to U.S. territories (Puerto Rico, Guam, U.S. Virgin Islands).

International availability may expand in the future. Watch the announcement bar at the top of this site for updates.

Section 8

Shipping Costs

Shipping cost is set by the individual seller and displayed on each product listing before checkout. Some sellers offer free shipping above a stated order minimum. The total — including shipping and any applicable platform fees — is shown clearly before you confirm payment. Silque Road never adds hidden fees at checkout.

Section 9

Address Accuracy

Buyers are responsible for providing an accurate shipping address at checkout. Once an order is dispatched, the address cannot be changed. If a package is returned to the seller due to an incorrect or undeliverable address, the buyer is responsible for the cost of reshipment.

If you spot a typo immediately after placing an order, email info@silqueroad.com with your Order ID right away. We can sometimes catch the order before the seller dispatches it, but cannot guarantee changes after the 1–3 business day processing window begins.

Section 10

Questions About a Specific Order

For any shipping question tied to a specific order, email info@silqueroad.com with your Order ID. We respond within 1–2 business days. For general questions about how shipping works on Silque Road, the contact form is the fastest way to reach us.
