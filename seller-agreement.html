01 Agreement Overview

This Seller Agreement ("Agreement") governs your participation as a seller on the Silque Road marketplace platform operated by XEKIE LLC. By submitting a seller application, accepting approval, or listing any product on Silque Road, you ("Seller") agree to be bound by this Agreement in its entirety.

This Agreement incorporates by reference the Silque Road Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Silque Road exists because mainstream platforms fail legal sellers. We will never deplatform a seller for selling a legal product.

02 Seller Eligibility & Vetting

To sell on Silque Road, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age or a legally formed business entity
  • Be legally permitted to sell the products you wish to list
  • Hold any licenses, permits, or certifications required by law for your product category
  • Operate in good faith and without intent to defraud buyers
  • Submit a complete and truthful seller application

Silque Road reserves the right to approve or deny any seller application at its sole discretion. Providing false information in a seller application is grounds for immediate and permanent termination.

03 Permitted Products

Permitted categories currently include:

  • Health freedom products (ivermectin, peptides, methylene blue, fenbendazole, and similar)
  • Psychedelic-adjacent botanicals (kratom, amanita muscaria, kava, blue lotus, salvia — where legal)
  • Cannabis-adjacent products (Delta-8 THC, THCA, HHC, CBN, hemp flower — where legal)
  • Longevity and biohacking supplements (NAD+, BPC-157, shilajit, nootropics)
  • Privacy and communications products (burner phones, faraday bags, counter-surveillance tools)
  • Legal weapons-adjacent products (blades, stun guns, fireworks — where legal)
  • Men's health and women's wellness products
  • Adult wellness products (age-verified)
  • Taboo foods (raw dairy, exotic meats, high-proof spirits — where legal)
  • Tobacco and nicotine products (where legal and properly labeled)
  • Off-grid, prepper, and homestead products

04 Prohibited Products

The following are strictly prohibited regardless of claimed legality:

  • Controlled substances under the federal Controlled Substances Act (Schedule I–V) without valid prescription fulfillment framework
  • Prescription medications sold without a valid prescription from a licensed provider
  • Firearms or components regulated under the National Firearms Act
  • Products containing undisclosed or mislabeled active pharmaceutical ingredients
  • Fraudulently described, mislabeled, or misrepresented products
  • Products intended to facilitate harm to another person
  • Counterfeit goods or goods infringing on third-party intellectual property

05 Seller Obligations

As an approved seller, you agree to:

  • List only products you are legally authorized to sell and ship
  • Accurately describe all products including ingredients, intended use, and required disclaimers
  • Tag all products with accurate state availability and prohibited states information
  • Ship only to buyers in states where your product is legal
  • Fulfill orders promptly and in compliance with all applicable shipping laws
  • Maintain a cryptocurrency wallet capable of receiving payouts
  • Notify Silque Road promptly if any product's legal status changes
  • Maintain any required licenses and permits in good standing

06 Commission & Payouts

Standard commission structure:

Gross Sale Price100%
NOWPayments Processing Fee0.5%
Platform Commission (Standard)10%
Seller Payout90% of sale price

Commission rates may be negotiated for high-volume sellers. Payouts are issued in cryptocurrency to the wallet address on file in your seller account on a weekly basis.

07 Fulfillment & Shipping

You, the seller, are solely responsible for order fulfillment. Silque Road does not warehouse, handle, or ship products. You agree to ship orders within your stated processing time, package products in compliance with applicable regulations, and not ship products to states where they are prohibited.

Disputes arising from shipping failures are between you and the buyer. Silque Road is not a party to the shipping contract between you and your carrier.

08 Legal Compliance

You are solely responsible for ensuring that every product you sell is legal under all applicable federal, state, and local laws in the jurisdictions to which you ship. Silque Road does not provide legal advice and does not verify the legality of your products in every jurisdiction. It is your obligation to stay current on applicable laws and update your listings accordingly.

Silque Road reserves the right to remove any listing at any time if we have reason to believe it may violate applicable law, without liability to the seller.

09 Representations & Warranties

By listing on Silque Road, you represent and warrant that:

  • You have full legal authority to sell and ship the products you list
  • All product descriptions, ingredient lists, and claims are accurate and not misleading
  • Your products comply with all applicable federal, state, and local regulations
  • You hold all required licenses and permits in good standing
  • Your products do not infringe on any third-party intellectual property rights
  • All information provided in your seller application is true and accurate

10 Indemnification

You agree to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless XEKIE LLC from and against any claims, damages, losses, and expenses (including attorneys' fees) arising from your products, your violation of this Agreement or applicable law, any dispute between you and a buyer, or any false representation made in your seller application or product listings.

11 Termination

XEKIE LLC will never terminate a seller account solely because a product category receives negative attention from third parties or is deplatformed elsewhere — provided the products remain legal.

XEKIE LLC reserves the right to suspend or terminate a seller account immediately for: listing prohibited products, providing false information, fraudulent conduct toward buyers, failure to fulfill orders, any conduct creating legal liability for XEKIE LLC, or violation of applicable law.

Upon termination, pending payouts for confirmed orders will be processed. Payouts for orders in dispute at termination may be held pending resolution.

12 Disputes

Disputes between sellers and buyers are primarily the seller's responsibility to resolve. Any dispute between a seller and XEKIE LLC shall be governed by the laws of the State of Ohio and subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of courts located in Ohio.
