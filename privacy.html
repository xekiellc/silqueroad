Our Privacy Commitment

Silque Road is built on anonymity. We collect as little as possible, use it only to fulfill your order, and sell none of it — ever. No cookies. No tracking pixels. No analytics. You have the right to shop without being watched.

01 Who We Are

Silque Road is operated by XEKIE LLC, an Ohio limited liability company. We operate the anonymous marketplace at silqueroad.com. For privacy inquiries, contact us at info@silqueroad.com.

02 What We Collect

We collect only what is strictly necessary to operate the platform. All fields at checkout are optional. Here is precisely what may be collected:

Data TypeWhen CollectedWhy
Email addressOptional at checkout or newsletter signupOrder updates or newsletter delivery
Shipping name & addressOptional at checkoutOrder fulfillment only
Cryptocurrency transaction IDGenerated at paymentOrder verification and records
Product and order detailsAt purchaseFulfillment and seller payment
Seller application dataOn seller application submissionSeller vetting and account creation

If you choose to provide no personal information, you may still complete a purchase using only cryptocurrency. Your identity is not required to shop on Silque Road.

03 What We Don't Collect

We have deliberately built this platform to avoid collecting the following:

  • Cookies of any kind — tracking, functional, or session
  • Browser fingerprints or device identifiers
  • IP addresses stored beyond the duration of a server request
  • Third-party analytics data (no Google Analytics, no Meta Pixel, no similar tools)
  • Behavioral data, browsing history, or click patterns
  • Social media login credentials or social profile data
  • Payment card numbers (processed entirely by third-party processors)

✓ No tracking. No cookies. No analytics. This is not a disclaimer buried in fine print — it is a design decision made before writing a single line of code.

04 How We Use Information

Any information you provide is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Fulfilling your order and communicating with your seller
  • Sending order status updates if you provided an email
  • Delivering newsletter content if you subscribed
  • Calculating and distributing seller payouts
  • Maintaining records required for business operations and legal compliance
  • Vetting seller applications and managing seller accounts

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, remarketing, or any purpose beyond what is listed above.

05 Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. Period.

We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your seller: Shipping name and address are shared with the seller who fulfills your order.
  • With payment processors: Transaction data is shared with NOWPayments or card processors solely to process your payment.
  • With email service providers: Email address is shared with Brevo or Resend solely to deliver transactional or newsletter emails.
  • As required by law: We will disclose information if compelled by a valid court order, subpoena, or lawful government request. We will notify affected users where legally permitted to do so.

06 Third-Party Services

Silque Road uses the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — database hosting
  • Netlify — website hosting and serverless functions
  • NOWPayments — cryptocurrency payment processing
  • Brevo — transactional email delivery
  • Resend — newsletter email delivery
  • Cloudflare — content delivery and DNS

We do not authorize these providers to use your data for any purpose other than providing services to Silque Road.

07 Data Retention

We retain order records, including any shipping information provided, for a minimum of three years to comply with business record-keeping requirements. Newsletter subscriber information is retained until you unsubscribe.

If you provided no personal information during your purchase, no personally identifiable record of your transaction exists beyond the blockchain record of your cryptocurrency payment.

08 Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To make a data request, email us at info@silqueroad.com with the subject line "Data Request." We will respond within 30 days.

Newsletter subscribers may unsubscribe at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

09 Security

We implement industry-standard security practices including encrypted database storage via Supabase, HTTPS enforced across all pages via Cloudflare, and row-level security policies on all database tables. Access to order and seller data is restricted to authorized platform functions only.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we take commercially reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security of any information transmitted to or stored on our platform.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted to this page with an updated effective date. We will not reduce your privacy protections without prominent notice.
