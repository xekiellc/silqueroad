03 What We Don't Collect

We have deliberately built this platform to avoid collecting the following:

  • Browser fingerprints or device identifiers
  • IP addresses stored beyond the duration of a server request
  • Third-party analytics data (no Google Analytics, no Meta Pixel, no similar tools)
  • Behavioral data, browsing history, or click patterns
  • Social media login credentials or social profile data
  • Payment card numbers (processed entirely by third-party processors — we never see or store them)

✓ Privacy-first is not a marketing line — it is a design decision made before writing a single line of code. We minimize data collection at every level of the platform.

04 Age Verification Data

Silque Road requires all visitors to confirm they are 18 years of age or older before accessing the platform. This confirmation is stored as a browser cookie (sr_age_18_verified) that expires after 30 days. This cookie contains no personal information — only the value "true" to prevent the age gate from reappearing on each page visit.

Certain product categories (Tobacco & Nicotine, Taboo Foods, Cannabis Adjacent, Psychedelic Adjacent, Weapons Adjacent) may require a separate 21+ age confirmation at checkout, stored as a session-only cookie (sr_age_21_verified) that expires when you close your browser.

These are the only cookies used on this platform. They are functional cookies required for legal compliance — not tracking cookies.

05 How We Use Information

Any information you provide is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Fulfilling your order and communicating with your seller
  • Sending order status updates and tracking information if you provided an email
  • Responding to support inquiries submitted via the contact form or email
  • Processing refunds, returns, and dispute mediation
  • Calculating and distributing seller payouts
  • Maintaining records required for business operations and legal compliance
  • Vetting seller applications and managing seller accounts

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, remarketing, behavioral targeting, or any purpose beyond what is listed above.

06 Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. Period.

We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your seller: Shipping name and address (if provided) are shared with the seller who fulfills your order. No other personal information is shared with sellers.
  • With payment processors: Transaction data is shared with NOWPayments (crypto) or Bankful (card) solely to process your payment. We never transmit card numbers — those go directly to the processor.
  • With email service providers: Email address (if provided) is shared with Resend solely to deliver transactional order emails.
  • With hosting providers: Order data is stored in Supabase (database) and processed through Netlify (serverless functions). Both operate under their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.
  • As required by law: We will disclose information if compelled by a valid court order, subpoena, or lawful government request. We will notify affected users where legally permitted to do so.

07 Third-Party Services

Silque Road uses the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — database hosting and authentication
  • Netlify — website hosting, serverless functions, and form processing
  • NOWPayments — cryptocurrency payment processing
  • Bankful — credit/debit card payment processing (pending approval)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Cloudflare — content delivery, DNS, and SSL

We do not authorize these providers to use your data for any purpose other than providing services to Silque Road. We do not share data with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing platforms.

08 Payment & Financial Data

Cryptocurrency payments: When you pay with cryptocurrency, your payment is processed by NOWPayments. We receive a transaction confirmation and invoice ID but do not have access to your wallet address, private keys, or blockchain identity. The transaction is recorded on the public blockchain.

Card payments: When you pay by credit or debit card, your payment is processed by Bankful. Card numbers are transmitted directly to the processor and never pass through or are stored on Silque Road's servers. Charges will appear on your statement as SILQUEROAD.

Silque Road, LLC does not store credit card numbers, CVV codes, or full card details at any point in the transaction.

09 Seller Information

Sellers who apply to the platform submit business information including legal name, business type, product descriptions, contact email, and cryptocurrency wallet addresses for payouts. This information is used exclusively for seller vetting, account management, and payout processing.

Seller information is not publicly displayed beyond the seller's chosen display name and product listings. Seller legal names, wallet addresses, and application details are kept confidential and are not shared with buyers or other sellers.

10 Data Retention

We retain order records, including any shipping information provided, for a minimum of three years to comply with business record-keeping and tax requirements. Contact form submissions are retained for one year.

Seller account data is retained for the duration of the seller's active account plus three years after account closure.

If you provided no personal information during your purchase, no personally identifiable record of your transaction exists beyond the blockchain record of your cryptocurrency payment (if applicable) and the internal Order ID.

You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 11: Your Rights). Certain records may be retained as required by law.

11 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of any personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Portability — request your data in a standard machine-readable format

To make a data request, email info@silqueroad.com with the subject line "Data Request" or use the contact form and select "Legal or Compliance" as the topic. We will respond within 30 days.

12 Security

We implement industry-standard security practices including encrypted database storage via Supabase, HTTPS enforced across all pages via Cloudflare, row-level security policies on all database tables, and access controls restricting order and seller data to authorized platform functions only.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we take commercially reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security of any information transmitted to or stored on our platform.

If we become aware of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify affected users by email (if an email is on file) and post a notice on the platform within 72 hours of discovery.

13 Children's Privacy

Silque Road is intended exclusively for adults 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you are under 18, do not use this platform, do not provide any information, and do not attempt to make a purchase.

If we learn that we have collected personal information from a person under 18, we will delete that information immediately. If you believe a minor has provided information to us, please contact info@silqueroad.com.
