Returns & Refunds

Fair, transparent, and built for a marketplace of independent sellers — with clear timelines and no surprises.

Last Updated: April 15, 2026

Section 1

The Basics

Silque Road is a marketplace. Each product is sold by an independent vetted seller, not by Silque Road, LLC directly. When something goes wrong with an order, the platform mediates between buyer and seller to make it right.

You have 14 days from delivery to open a return, refund, or replacement request. After 14 days, all sales are considered final unless a manufacturing defect surfaces and is documented.

The shortest version: If your order arrives wrong, broken, or never shows up — email info@silqueroad.com with your Order ID. We'll fix it.

Section 2

What Qualifies for a Refund

The following situations qualify for a full refund or replacement, at the buyer's choice:

  • Wrong item shipped. You received a different product than what you ordered.
  • Damaged in transit. Item arrived broken, leaking, crushed, or otherwise unusable. Photos required within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Defective on arrival. Item does not function as described or has a manufacturing defect documented within 14 days.
  • Never delivered. Tracking shows no delivery, or movement stopped 10+ business days ago with no resolution from the carrier.
  • Significantly not as described. The item differs materially from the listing — wrong size, wrong quantity, wrong potency, expired, etc.
  • Seller cannot lawfully ship. If a seller cannot ship to your state or address due to legal restrictions, your order is refunded automatically.

Section 3

What Does Not Qualify

For health, safety, and category-specific reasons, the following are final sale and not eligible for return:

  • Opened consumables. Any food, supplement, peptide, tincture, kratom, herbal product, or ingestible item once the seal is broken.
  • Personal-use items. Adult products, intimacy items, smoking accessories, and similar categories once removed from sealed packaging.
  • Perishables. Raw dairy, organ meats, fermented foods, and other perishables, regardless of seal status.
  • Custom or made-to-order items. Anything produced specifically for your order.
  • Buyer's remorse. "Changed my mind" is not a qualifying reason once the item ships.
  • Delivery to wrong address. If you provided an incorrect address at checkout, you are responsible for the cost of reshipment.

Important: If you receive a defective or contaminated consumable, do not consume it. Photograph the product and seal, then contact us within 48 hours. We will pursue a refund or replacement on your behalf even though opened consumables are normally non-returnable.

Section 4

How to Request a Refund

Email info@silqueroad.com with the subject line "Refund Request — [Order ID]" and include:

  1. Your Order ID (shown at checkout and on any order email).
  2. The reason for the refund (one of the qualifying categories above).
  3. Clear photos of the product, packaging, and shipping label if the issue is damage, wrong item, or defect.
  4. Your preferred resolution — refund or replacement.

We acknowledge every refund request within 1–2 business days. Most are resolved within 5–7 business days. Complex cases (carrier investigations, seller disputes) can take up to 14 business days.

Section 5

Refund Methods & Timing

Refunds are issued in the same payment method used at checkout.

Payment Method Refund Method Timing After Approval
Credit / Debit Card Refund to original card 5–10 business days to appear on statement
Cryptocurrency Refund in same cryptocurrency, sent to a wallet address you provide 1–3 business days after wallet confirmation

About crypto refunds: Crypto values fluctuate. Refunds are issued at the USD equivalent of your original purchase, converted to crypto at the time of refund. You may receive a slightly different number of coins than you sent. Network fees are deducted from the refund amount.

Section 6

Returning the Physical Item

For most defect or wrong-item claims, we do not require you to ship the item back. Photo evidence is enough. This keeps the process fast and saves you the cost and hassle of return shipping.

If a seller specifically requests the item be returned (rare — usually for high-value goods), Silque Road will provide a prepaid return label. Do not ship anything back at your own expense without prior written confirmation from us.

Section 7

Order Cancellations

You may cancel an order for a full refund at any time before the seller marks it as dispatched, which is typically within the 1–3 business day processing window. Email info@silqueroad.com with your Order ID immediately.

Once an order has shipped, it cannot be cancelled. You may refuse delivery and request a refund once the package returns to the seller, less original shipping cost.

Section 8

Chargebacks

If you have a problem with an order, please contact us first. Most issues can be resolved within 5–7 business days through the refund process described above.

Initiating a chargeback with your card issuer before contacting us delays resolution and may result in your account being flagged for review. Fraudulent chargebacks — disputing a charge for an item you received and kept — may result in permanent ban from the platform and referral to the card network's fraud database.

Section 9

Anonymous Orders

If you checked out anonymously and provided no email at checkout, your Order ID is the only way to request a refund. Save it. Without it, we have no way to look up your order or verify the purchase.

For refunds on anonymous crypto orders, you will need to provide a wallet address to receive the refund. We will never ask for your private key, seed phrase, or wallet password — only the public address.

Section 10

Disputes Between Buyers & Sellers

Silque Road, LLC mediates all refund and return disputes. Our goal is fair resolution that protects both buyers and sellers. We review evidence from both sides — photos, tracking, listing description, communication history — and make a final determination within 14 business days of opening a dispute.

Decisions by Silque Road, LLC in mediated disputes are final.

Section 11

Questions

For any question about a specific order, refund, or return, email info@silqueroad.com with your Order ID. For general questions about how returns work on Silque Road, the contact form is the fastest way to reach us.
